FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about my thumbnail design services.

How long does it take to complete a thumbnail?

For a single thumbnail, I typically deliver within 24 hours. For larger packages, delivery times vary from 48-72 hours depending on complexity and revisions needed.

Do you provide source files?

Yes! All packages include the source files (PSD or AI) so you can make minor adjustments yourself or have them ready for future designers.

What information do you need from me?

To create the perfect thumbnail, I'll need your video topic, any specific images/elements you want included, your channel style/branding, and examples of thumbnails you like.

How many revisions are included?

The number of revision rounds depends on your package: Single Thumbnail (1 revision), Thumbnail Pro Pack (2 revisions), and Channel Booster (3 revisions).
